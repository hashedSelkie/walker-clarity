About

Abigail completed her Neuroscience BSc in 2016, her Brain Sciences MSc in 2018, and her Nutrition & Behaviour MSc in 2021. She is also a published and award-winning creative writer specialising in poetry and short stories.

In 2021 Abigail launched Walker Clarity to combine her academic credentials with creative and communicative writing, building a client base including large biotechnology companies, start-up food brands, and online health resources.
